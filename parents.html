For Parents

Instructions

Has your child gotten bored of just reading? Has the iPad been the go-to solution to occupy your child? Look no further than readUp, a new interactive reading game designed to promote literacy among early readers. We hope that with a game-like structure, not only will your child read, they will be excited about doing so.

The tracking method is as follows: Either you or your child will record information for a book they have spent reading 15 minutes or more in a day. Simply enter in a book title in the text box, and then click on the next stamp icon in the sequence to record the information. Once your child reaches 5 stamps, they will advance to the next level.

Our Mission

Research has shown that the recommended reading time for beginners is 15-20 minutes a day, and that reading comprehension is a crucial skill for development and later success in life. Yet, this can be difficult to achieve in young readers who demonstrate little interest in reading. At readUp, we believe that reading can, and should, be fun. Through our online interactive reading tracker, we hope to promote and inspire literacy at a young age, setting children up for successful lifelong learning.
